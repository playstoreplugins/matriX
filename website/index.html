Frequently Asked Questions

Is PlaystorePlugins free?

Yes, PlaystorePlugins is completely free to download and use. No hidden fees or subscriptions.

Does it work with the official TikTok app?

Yes, PlaystorePlugins works alongside the official TikTok app. You need to have TikTok installed on your device.

Will this drain my battery?

The app is optimized for battery efficiency. While keeping the screen on will use more power than normal, our smart power management minimizes the impact.

Is it safe to use?

Absolutely. PlaystorePlugins doesn't collect any personal data or track your activity. It simply manages screen and background settings.

Why isn't there an iOS version on the App Store?

Due to Apple's strict App Store policies, we provide the source code for iOS users to build the app themselves using Xcode.
